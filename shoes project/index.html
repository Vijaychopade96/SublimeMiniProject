About us

We, at Futstep Professional Shoe Care, strive to offer the best treatment to every pair of shoes and help revive their look. Futstep, which was started by Mr. Amit Pandey in 2009 has revived more than 1,50,000 pairs of shoes and offered its services to at least 25,000 shoe lovers during the past 11 years. At the Futstep facility, every shoe is handled by shoe experts who are thoroughly trained to handle high-end luxury and branded sneakers. Futstep experts are trained to handle services like shoe dry cleaning, mending, customizing, repairing, premium polishing, leather restoration, color correction, anti-fungal treatment, and deodorizing services. Once ready, every pair of shoes passes through a detailed quality check just before the dispatch. The best part is that you can book the service online and get a pick-up and drop at your location which makes it an exceptionally convenient way to give a fresh new look to your shoes. So, Let’s make your shoes spick and span!

Frequently Asked Questions.

What all is included in shoe cleaning service?

Cleaning services at Futstep includes the following:

  1. Deep or dry cleaning the shoes from inside and outside.
  2. Cleaning the laces and insoles separately.
  3. Drying it completely from inside and outside.
  4. Applying shoe perfume specifically to the inner area of shoes.
  5. Final finishing to clear away any tough stains or marks.
  6. Anti-dust packaging to keep them safe till they are delivered to you.

Please note that every shoe make is different and hence, the cleaning process applied on them differs as well.

How do i book a service?

Futstep professional shoe care services are available in major Indian cities. Just fill-up the service inquiry form with correct details and our team will get back to you with a quote and service timeline. You can even share your details over WhatsApp on to our official helpline numbers +91 7720071777 (Mumbai and Rest of India) and +91 8888807475 (Nashik). Our team would be glad to help you restore your shoes.

What all repairs you do?

We do almost all minor and major repair work on your shoes to bring them back to life. We do:

  • Press Pasting work for outsoles.
  • Machine stitch work.
  • Press pasting for the minor opening of soles.
  • Minor touch-ups using professional shoe paint.
  • Counter heel cushion repair.
  • Minor and major patchwork for upper mesh.
  • Resoling of leather shoes.
  • Replacing insoles and laces with new ones.

If you still feel that your shoe has a problem apart from these, please feel free to connect with our shoe experts by simply sharing clear images of the damaged portion of your shoes on our official WhatsApp number. Our team will offer you the best available solution along with a quote and timeline.

What is the guarantee or warranty for the repair service that you offer?

We don’t offer any sort of assurance, guarantee, or warranty for any repairs done from our end. Our motive is to offer a solution to your used shoes so that their durability improves. It may or may not be possible to restore them fully as the shoes are used under uncontrolled and untraced circumstances. However, our method of repairs is of top quality. Despite this, if we fall short of your expectation, We do commit to try our best to save your shoes as much as possible.

Do you provide pick-up and drop service in my area?


We offer pick-up and drop services across major Indian cities via our logistics partner. Please feel free to share your pin-code details along with your requirement and our team would instantly offer you a service detail along with a quote and service timeline for your location.

My shoes are expensive. What if my shoe gets damaged during the process?

We understand your concern completely and that’s the reason we have built a flawless system to avoid any damage to your valuable shoes. However, in a scenario where humans are involved, there might be a rare and unavoidable scenario where the loss of footwear or damage may happen. To secure your shoes against such unfortunate events, we cover your items and pay only up to 5 times the service amount (excluding the logistics charges) as per the standard laundry industry policy. In addition to that, we would like to inform you that we have been in the professional shoe care business for the past 11 years now. All the items are handled by trained experts at Futstep facility. Hence, you can stay assured of the safety of your valuable footwear with us.

How do i pay for my services?

Please note that we have an advance payment policy. You can make payments online via IMPS or G-pay/Phonepe once you receive the quotation for your items. We will start the work on the shoes only after receiving the full payment confirmation. This will also help us keep the delivery process smooth. Any delay in sharing payment confirmation may lead to a delay in delivery.
NEFT / PAYTM / CHEQUE are strictly unacceptable and won’t be considered as paid. We have stopped accepting any cash payments.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation charges are applicable once the order is picked up from your location and then canceled by either you or us due to any technical reason or infeasibility to work on the items. There are situations when images shared by customers are not clear for us to understand the feasibility of work. In such scenarios, we only have an option to share a final quote once we receive the shoes at our workshop. Cancellation charges or Visiting charges are only aimed to cover the logistics and processing expense incurred by Futstep. Users can pay online and share the transaction details after which their items are dispatched in undone condition.

How much is the turnaround time?

If you choose a regular service, the shoe cleaning takes approximately 2-3 days and if you opt for express service, we deliver it back either on the same day or the next day. Please note that depending on your location, express service may or may not be available and the timeline of other services may vary. It is highly recommended that you share all the details properly to get an exact timeline and charges from our team. The additional charges for express delivery range from INR 200/- to INR 250/- depending on your location and service requirement.

I need to know the exact charges of servicing my shoes before i submit them.

The best practice is to share clear images of the damaged portion of your shoes with our team and list down the service requirements so that our team can evaluate them and get back to you with an approximate quotation and timeline. Once you confirm the quote, we can immediately get your shoes picked up from your location. After receiving the shoes at our workshop, a final quality check is done to ensure the feasibility of the work. Thereafter, we share a final quotation including all the job details and specifications. Once you approve it, we begin the work on your shoes. You still have an option to decline the offered quote and recall your shoes by just paying the minimum cancellation or visiting charges.

Do i have an option to keep a different pick up and a different drop address for my order?


Yes, you can surely do that. You can request a pick up from address A and request delivery at address B. In addition to that, we recommend you check the feasibility and additional charges at both locations with our shoe care team before the booking process.

I want to visit your shop or workshop at Mumbai?


Futstep services are only available online. We don’t invite customers to our workshop as per our company’s policy. To serve you in a better manner, we have our workshops at various locations in Mumbai and we service across major Indian cities through our network of logistics partners. You can request a seamless pick-up & drop service at your address. Please be rest assured that you will get all the support required by our shoe care team.

How do i raise a complaint if i am not convinced with the service?

If you wish to share feedback or genuinely feel that you can get better results on your items, please highlight it to us through an email at enquiry@futstep.in and make sure that you attach images to help us understand your concern in a better manner. Our senior team generally gets back to such e-mails on top priority and will revert with the best solution within 2 working days. In addition to this, kindly note that we don’t have a refund policy if we are not able to achieve a 100% result on your shoes.
