About Us

Vision

At cobbler’s magic, our goal is to provide highest quality shoe cleaning and repairing services to our consumers with greatest ease. We want to completely change the traditional approach of washing the shoes at home and replace the same with professional services where our shoe experts will take care of your shoes with all the latest shoe products and tools. When a shoe is professionally cleaned using right set of tools and products, it increases the life of a shoe. At the same time, we have made this shoe washing/repairing experience so smooth and effortless that the consumer is just few clicks away from ordering these services and the rest is taken care by us – right from picking up the shoes from consumer to the delivery to shoes after the shoes have been serviced. While providing the shoe washing/repairing services, we also strive at spreading the awareness about footwear hygiene. Hygiene, as definition says, is a series of practices that preserve health. One cannot ignore footwear hygiene when it comes to maintaining a total body health. When we use a footwear, it picks up bacteria and tiny microorganisms as we walk on the streets, offices, parks, toilets etc. Also, the inside of the footwear is subjected to sweat where all the sweat is soaked into the footwear. A daily used footwear should be washed once every two weeks. However, depending upon the type of the footwear and how often it is used, this washing interval might vary from once every two weeks to once every two months. This is very important for all the athletes and fitness enthusiasts who use their sports shoes almost everyday for their workout. Opting to avail shoe washing/repairing services from Cobbler’s Magic will take care of the footwear hygiene for all and promote better, healthier practices in our day to day living in order to ensure good health.
